A homelab becomes much easier to maintain when code, stateful data, and runtime services do not all live in the same mental bucket.

## A more coherent structure

I prefer to think in three layers:

- repositories and source code,
- persistent data and volumes,
- running services and automation.

That separation reduces confusion, improves backup logic, and makes troubleshooting faster.
