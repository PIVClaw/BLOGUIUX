For a long time, the temptation was to search for the one model that could do everything. In reality, the more I worked on real use cases, the more obvious it became that different tasks deserve different strengths.

## Why multi-model makes sense

A personal assistant does not just answer questions. It may need to:

- write clearly,
- reason carefully,
- follow tools reliably,
- summarize quickly,
- and adapt to different latency or cost constraints.

This is where a multi-model strategy becomes practical rather than theoretical.
