One of the easiest ways to create chaos in a personal knowledge system is to let multiple tools point to slightly different paths, copies, or assumptions.

## The real challenge

The challenge was not just storing notes. It was making sure:

- the vault exposed through Samba,
- the files indexed into Qdrant,
- and the notes edited in Obsidian

were all referring to the same source of truth.

That sounds obvious, but small path mismatches can silently break the whole pipeline.
