A blog is more than a place to publish articles. It is a space to think in public, document experiments, and build long-term credibility.

## Why now

A strong blog helps you:

- clarify your ideas,
- demonstrate your thinking,
- compound your experience,
- create durable visibility.

## The direction

The goal here is simple: build a modern, bilingual, premium blog designed to last.
